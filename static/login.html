The Architecture

Precision.
Automated.

AUREX transcends basic generative imagery. It orchestrates a cascade of reasoning models — starting with DeepSeek R1 — to author exact mathematical geometry via headless Blender.

The result is not a flat image, but true volumetric mesh geometry. Inspect it from every angle, edit it in your software of choice, or deploy it instantly to the web.

The Pipeline

Five Stages.
Total Control.

01
Enhance
DeepSeek R1 expands your prompt, analyzing structure, material properties, and complexity requirements.
02
Script
The LLM authors a complete Blender Python script, utilizing primitives and booleans to construct your object.
03
Render
Blender 4.2 executes the script headlessly, validating meshes and exporting a lightweight GLB artifact.
04
Persist
The model is uploaded to Cloudinary CDN and logged into your isolated Supabase history.
05
View
Three.js ingests the GLB. Interact natively with mouse, touch, or MediaPipe hand gestures.
V8.0 Capabilities

Built for scale.

Multi-Variant Generation
Generate three parallel interpretations of your prompt simultaneously. Compare and select the optimal geometry instantly.
Image-to-3D
Upload reference imagery. Gemini Vision analyzes the visual data and translates it into a detailed architectural prompt.
Kinematic Gestures
Navigate the 3D viewport using MediaPipe hand tracking. Featuring One Euro filtering for silky, inertia-driven movement.
Supabase Persistence
Google OAuth secures your session. Every generated model is saved to your personal, isolated Postgres history.
Fail-Safe Cascade
DeepSeek R1 fails? Fallback to Gemini 2.0 Flash. Falls back to Groq LLaMA 3.3. Falls back to 54 presets. You always get a model.
Universal Export
Download native GLB for web deployment, or instantly convert to OBJ and FBX formats via headless Blender servers.
